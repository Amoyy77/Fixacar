About FixaCar

At FixaCar, we’re more than just a car dealership – we’re your partners in the journey of finding the perfect vehicle. Whether you're buying your first car, upgrading your ride, or looking for something reliable for your family, we’ve got you covered.

Founded with a passion for quality vehicles and exceptional customer service, FixaCar has grown into a trusted name in the automotive industry. We proudly offer a wide selection of certified pre-owned vehicles, foreign used cars, and local trade-ins, all carefully inspected and priced with transparency.

Our Mission

"To drive value, trust, and satisfaction in every vehicle we sell, by delivering quality cars and personalized service that exceed expectations."

At FixaCar, our mission fuels everything we do. We believe everyone deserves access to a reliable vehicle and a buying experience that’s simple, honest, and stress-free.
